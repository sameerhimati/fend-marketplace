Payment Terms

Payment structure, fees, and billing terms for Platform services.

1. Subscription Fees

Platform access requires an active subscription. Fees are charged monthly or annually in advance, depending on your selected plan.

Enterprise Plans

  • Monthly: $100 per month (5 pilots included)
  • Annual: $1,000 per year (unlimited pilots, save $200)

Startup Plans

  • Monthly: $10 per month
  • Annual: $100 per year (save $20)

2. Pilot Program Fees

In addition to subscription fees, pilot programs may incur transaction-based fees:

  • Payment processing fee: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
  • Platform service fee: 5% of pilot contract value
  • Escrow service fee: 1% of total pilot amount

3. Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • ACH bank transfers (US accounts only)
  • Wire transfers for enterprise customers
  • Corporate procurement for qualified organizations

4. Billing Cycle and Due Dates

  • Monthly subscriptions are billed on the same day each month
  • Annual subscriptions are billed on the anniversary date
  • Pilot fees are charged upon contract execution
  • All payments are due immediately upon invoicing

5. Late Payments and Penalties

Late payment terms:

  • Grace period: 5 business days from due date
  • Late fee: $25 or 1.5% of outstanding amount (whichever is greater)
  • Service suspension: After 15 days past due
  • Account termination: After 30 days past due

6. Refunds and Credits

Our refund policy:

  • Subscription fees are generally non-refundable
  • Unused pilot funds may be refunded upon request
  • Service credits may be issued for platform downtime
  • Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days

7. Tax Responsibilities

Users are responsible for applicable taxes:

  • Sales tax will be added where required by law
  • International users responsible for VAT and local taxes
  • Tax-exempt organizations must provide valid documentation

8. Payment Processing

Payment processing details:

  • Payments processed securely through Stripe and other certified providers
  • Credit card information is encrypted and not stored on our servers
  • Failed payments will trigger automatic retry attempts

9. Pilot Escrow Services

For pilot programs, we provide secure escrow services:

  • Funds are held in FDIC-insured accounts
  • Release upon milestone completion and verification
  • Dispute resolution for contested deliverables

10. Changes to Payment Terms

We may modify payment terms with 30 days' notice. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of updated terms.
