User Agreement

Additional terms specific to user accounts, marketplace participation, and platform usage.

1. User Registration and Accounts

To access certain features of the Fend.ai platform, you must register for an account. By registering, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate and complete information
  • Maintain the security of your account credentials
  • Promptly update your information as needed
  • Accept responsibility for all activities under your account

2. Organization Types and Verification

Enterprise Organizations

Enterprise users participate as buyers in the Pilot Marketplace and may:

  • Browse and evaluate startup solutions
  • Initiate pilot programs with vetted startups
  • Access subscription-based features
  • Participate in enterprise feedback programs

Startup Organizations

Startup users participate as solution providers and may:

  • Submit solutions for marketplace listing
  • Participate in pilot programs with enterprises
  • Access feedback and development tools
  • Undergo verification processes

3. Pilot Marketplace Usage

When participating in pilot programs, users agree to:

  • Engage in good faith negotiations
  • Provide honest feedback and evaluations
  • Respect intellectual property rights
  • Maintain confidentiality as required
  • Follow platform payment and dispute processes

4. Fend Labs Beta Programs

Beta program participants agree to:

  • Provide constructive feedback on experimental features
  • Understand that beta features may be unstable
  • Not use beta features for production purposes
  • Maintain confidentiality regarding unreleased features

5. Content and Intellectual Property

Users retain ownership of content they submit but grant Fend.ai necessary rights to operate the platform. Users agree not to:

  • Infringe on others' intellectual property rights
  • Submit false or misleading information
  • Share confidential information without permission
  • Reverse engineer platform features

6. Payment and Subscription Terms

For paid services, users agree to:

  • Pay all applicable fees in a timely manner
  • Provide accurate billing information
  • Notify us of billing disputes within 60 days
  • Accept that subscription fees are non-refundable

Users may not:

  • Use automated systems to access the platform
  • Attempt to circumvent security measures

Users acknowledge that we may:

9. Account Suspension and Termination

10. Platform Modifications

We reserve the right to modify or discontinue platform features with notice. We will provide reasonable notice for material changes affecting paid services.

11. Support and Service Level

We provide support on a best-effort basis. Enterprise subscribers may receive priority support as outlined in their subscription terms.

12. Dispute Resolution

For disputes arising from platform use, users agree to first attempt resolution through our internal processes before pursuing legal action.

Users must comply with all applicable laws and regulations in their use of the platform, including but not limited to:
