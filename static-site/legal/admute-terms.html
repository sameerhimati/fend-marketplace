3. How Admute Works

3.1 Technology Description

Admute detects advertisement user interface elements in real-time and temporarily mutes system audio during advertisement playback. No content is modified, blocked, or skipped.

3.2 No Content Modification

Admute only controls audio output. We do not:

  • Block, hide, or remove advertisements
  • Skip or fast-forward content
  • Modify video streams or website code
  • Interfere with platform analytics or revenue

3.3 Local Processing

All detection processing occurs locally on your device. No user data or viewing information is transmitted to Fend servers.
